关于我About Me

我是哈尔滨工业大学海量数据计算研究中心博士生，导师为王宏志教授，副导师为丁小欧副教授。我的研究围绕 Data Quality for AI 展开，关注数据质量、数据构建与模型需求之间的关系。

I am a Ph.D. student at the Massive Data Computing Research Center, Harbin Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Hongzhi Wang and co-advised by Prof. Xiaoou Ding. My research focuses on Data Quality for AI, especially the interaction among data quality, data construction, and model demand.

我希望把数据处理决策做得更可解释、可复现，也更贴近具体 AI 任务：什么数据值得修，什么数据应该保留，怎样在预算受限时组织清洗、准备和模型构建。

I aim to make data-side decisions interpretable, reproducible, and useful for concrete AI tasks: what to repair, what to preserve, and how to organize cleaning, preparation, and model construction under limited budgets.

研究兴趣Research Interests

  • 面向人工智能的数据质量管理：分析数据错误、清洗动作与模型表现之间的关系。Data quality management for AI: understanding how errors and cleaning actions affect model behavior.
  • 面向人工智能的数据构建：组织数据准备流程、清洗算子和实验记录，使数据构建过程可复现、可比较。Data construction for AI: organizing preparation workflows, cleaning operators, and execution traces for reproducible data construction.
  • 质量预算下的机器学习与 AutoML：在清洗、标注、模型搜索和训练成本之间做联合决策。ML and AutoML under quality budgets: jointly allocating cleaning, labeling, model-search, and training costs.
