研究方向Research

我研究数据质量、数据构建与模型需求之间的关系，目标是让数据处理决策能够解释、复现，并服务具体 AI 任务。I study how data quality, data construction, and model demand interact, with an emphasis on interpretable and reproducible data decisions for AI tasks.

目前的工作集中在 Data Quality for AI。数据清洗并不总是越多越好；在模型、任务和预算都确定后，更需要回答的是哪些错误值得处理，哪些数据改动会破坏真实性或多样性，以及清洗流程能否被后续任务复用。

My work is centered on Data Quality for AI. More cleaning is not always better; once the task, model, and budget are fixed, the key questions are what to repair, what to preserve, and how preparation workflows can be reused across tasks.

01

面向人工智能的数据质量管理Data Quality for AI

研究数据质量问题如何影响 AI 模型，以及清洗、修复、删除、保留等动作应如何服务下游任务。代表工作是 DemandClean（VLDB 2025）。

I study how data quality issues affect AI models, and how cleaning, repair, deletion, and preservation decisions should serve downstream tasks. Representative work: DemandClean (VLDB 2025).

02

面向人工智能的数据构建Data construction for AI

研究如何为 AI 任务构建更可用的数据：从候选数据、清洗策略、算子组合到可复现的数据准备流程。对应开源的 DemandPrep artifact。

I study how to construct usable data for AI tasks, from candidate data and cleaning strategies to operator composition and reproducible preparation workflows. Released as the DemandPrep artifact.

03

质量预算下的 ML / AutoMLML and AutoML under quality budgets

当清洗预算、标注预算和模型搜索预算互相牵制时，需要联合决定数据质量提升与模型构建。DMCO（ICML 2026）研究两边的预算怎么分配。

When cleaning, labeling, and model-search budgets interact, data quality improvement and model construction should be optimized together. DMCO (ICML 2026) studies this allocation problem.

论文Publications

面向人工智能的数据质量管理、数据构建与数据治理方向的论文Papers on Data Quality for AI, data construction, and data governance

研究主题Research theme

这些论文主要围绕数据清洗、数据准备和数据治理展开。不同工作处理的问题不完全相同，但都关注同一类判断：数据处理动作是否真的改善了下游任务，以及这种改善需要付出多少成本。

These papers cover data cleaning, data preparation, and data governance. They ask related questions from different angles: whether a data-side action improves the downstream task, and what cost is paid for that improvement.
